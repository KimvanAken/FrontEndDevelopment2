Welcome to Neopets: Faerie's Hope!

  • Delve into a captivating Neopian world full of intriguing characters, daring adventures, and compelling storylines!
  • Build your own Faerieland in a casual, stress-free setting as you venture on an enchanting Neopian journey to help the lost Light Faerie find their way!
  • Engage in tantalising Match 3 gameplay while you embark on your journey to restore your home!

A Magical Island in the Sky

  • Construct your own slice of New Faerieland using nostalgic Faerieland colours and buildings!
  • Meet exciting new Neopian characters and reunite with classic Neopian figures to accompany you on your adventures!
  • With tons of unique building choices, diverse furnishing options, and stylish decorations, you can design your island in the sky however you like!

Relaxing and Engaging Gameplay

  • Thousands of engaging Match 3 puzzles with a plethora of vibrant, colourful arrangements ensure that you'll never want to leave Faerieland!
  • Kick back and relax in a world full of adorable icons, relaxing atmosphere, and refreshingly catchy music!

So, what are you waiting for? Start your adventure in New Faerieland today!
